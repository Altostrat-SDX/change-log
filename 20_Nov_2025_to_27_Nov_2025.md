This week, we're excited to roll out a major upgrade to our reporting engine, giving you powerful new ways to organize and schedule reports. We've also shipped key improvements to dashboard accuracy, scan reliability, and overall platform performance.

### üöÄ New Features

*   **Dynamic Report Scheduling with Tags:** You can now create report schedules that automatically include sites based on their tags. This is perfect for generating reports that adapt as your infrastructure changes, without needing manual updates.
*   **Group Report Data by Tags:** Organize sites within your reports by a specific tag, such as "Region" or "Customer," to see aggregated results for each group.
*   **New "Redundancy" Health Check for Grouped Sites:** A new aggregation option for grouped sites considers the group "online" if at least one site within it is active. This is ideal for accurately reporting on high-availability and clustered environments.

*   **Reporting:** We've completely overhauled our scheduled reporting system, making it significantly more reliable and scalable to ensure your reports are generated on time, every time.
*   **Dashboards & Visualizations:** Graphs on your dashboards are now more accurate, especially when you zoom in on specific time ranges, thanks to smarter query calculations.

*   **API:** Developers can now find the `username` field in the API response for tunnel resources, providing an additional useful identifier.
*   **Performance:** We've optimized how we collect device performance statistics (like CPU and memory), which improves overall platform speed and efficiency.
