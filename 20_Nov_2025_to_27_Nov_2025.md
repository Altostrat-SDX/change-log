Combined with dashboard accuracy improvements and enhanced vulnerability scanning, these updates help you manage distributed MikroTik networks more effectively.

- **Dynamic Tag-Based SLA Report Scheduling:** Create intelligent report schedules that automatically include sites based on tag-matching rules. As your network grows and sites are added with matching tags, they're automatically incorporated into scheduled reports without manual updates. Perfect for MSPs managing multiple customer networks or enterprises with dynamic network topologies.
- **Site Grouping by Tags in Reports:** Organize sites within SLA reports by specific tag dimensions such as `Region`, `Customer`, or `Priority` to view aggregated performance metrics for each logical group. This capability enables sophisticated multi-tenant reporting and hierarchical performance tracking.
- **Redundancy Health Calculation for High-Availability:** A new SLA aggregation method considers a group of sites "online" if at least one site within the group is operational. This is essential for accurately reporting uptime on high-availability clusters, redundant VPN hubs, and other fault-tolerant network architectures.
